Civil War in Derry June 1920

This simple web page details 3 different references to the mostly forgotten Civil War that unfolded in Derry in June 1920. Over a very short period 20 people were shot dead and 39 were wounded. These websites and short film give context and background from that time.

One of those killed 0n 19th June 1920 was my great grandfather James McVeigh who lies in this grave
